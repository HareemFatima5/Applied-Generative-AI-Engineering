# Summarization Prompts

## What is it?
Summarization prompting means asking the AI to take a long piece of text and shorten it while keeping the main meaning. We control how long the 
summary should be and who it is written for.

## How it Works
We give the AI the full text and tell it exactly how short the summary should be, for example 1 sentence, 2 sentences or 3 bullet points.

## When to Use it
- When we have a long article or report and need the key points only
- When we want a summary for a specific audience like a manager or a student
- When we need to save time reading long content

## Examples I Tried

### Example 1
**Prompt:**
Summarize the text below in exactly 2 sentences. Keep it simple for a general audience.

Text: Artificial intelligence is transforming industries worldwide. From healthcare 
to finance, AI systems are being used to analyze large amounts of data, detect 
patterns, and make predictions. In healthcare, AI helps doctors diagnose diseases 
earlier and more accurately. In finance, it detects fraud and manages risk. However, 
AI also raises concerns about job displacement and privacy.

**Output I got:**
Artificial intelligence is revolutionizing various sectors by analyzing data to 
improve medical diagnoses and financial security. While these advancements offer 
significant benefits, they also create important challenges regarding personal 
privacy and the future of jobs.


### Example 2 
**Prompt:**
Summarize the text below in exactly 1 sentence.

Text: The government announced a new policy today aimed at reducing plastic waste 
across the country. Under the new rules, single use plastic bags will be banned 
starting next year, and businesses will be encouraged to switch to biodegradable 
packaging. Officials say this step is part of a larger plan to tackle pollution 
and protect the environment.

**Output I got:**
The government has announced a new policy to combat pollution by banning 
single-use plastic bags next year and promoting the use of biodegradable packaging.

### Example 3 
**Prompt:**
You are summarizing this report for a busy manager who only has 30 seconds 
to read it. Summarize in 3 short bullet points, no more.

Text: Our quarterly sales report shows a 15% increase in revenue compared to 
last quarter, driven mainly by strong performance in the online segment. However, 
in-store sales dropped by 5% due to reduced foot traffic. The marketing team 
recommends increasing the digital ad budget for next quarter while reviewing 
the in-store experience to identify the cause of the decline.

**Output I got:**
- Revenue is up 15%: Growth is primarily driven by strong online sales.
- In-store decline: Physical store revenue dropped 5% due to lower foot traffic.
- Next steps: We will increase digital ad spend and investigate the root cause 
of the in-store decline.

# Zero-Shot Prompting

## What is it?
Zero-shot prompting means asking the AI to do a task without giving it any examples. We just describe what we want and the AI figures it out on its own using what it already knows.

## When to Use it
- When the task is simple and straightforward
- When we do not have examples ready
- When we want a quick answer

## Examples I tried

### Example 1 
**Prompt:**
"""Translate this sentence to Urdu
Sentence: I am learning AI"""

**Output I got:**
Zero shot prompting
You can translate "I am learning AI" into Urdu in a few ways:

1. **میں اے آئی (AI) سیکھ رہا ہوں۔** (If you are male)
2. **میں اے آئی (AI) سیکھ رہی ہوں۔** (If you are female)

### Example 2
**Prompt:**
"""Classify this review as Positive or Negative:
'The product was amazing and delivery was fast."""

**Output I got:**
Positive

### Example 3
**Prompt:**
"""Summarize this paragraph
Reading books is an incredibly rewarding habit that broadens our horizons and sharpens our minds."""

**Output I got:**
Reading books is a highly beneficial habit that expands knowledge and improves thinking.
